# Legal Compliance Guide for Fantasy Stats Subscription Website

## Step 1: Business Registration
1. Choose a business structure (e.g., LLC, Corporation).
2. Register your business name with the Secretary of State.
3. Obtain the necessary licenses and permits for your location.

## Step 2: Tax Setup
1. Apply for an EIN (Employer Identification Number) via the IRS website.
2. Determine your state tax obligations and register accordingly.
3. Set up sales tax collection if required for your products/services.

## Step 3: Banking
1. Open a business bank account.
2. Separate personal and business finances.
3. Manage your finances using accounting software or hire an accountant.

## Step 4: Legal Documents
1. Draft Terms of Service for your website.
2. Create a Privacy Policy that adheres to GDPR and CCPA regulations.
3. Consider legal agreements for partnerships or affiliates.

## Step 5: API Compliance
1. Ensure all third-party APIs comply with legal standards.
2. Review API terms of service and use agreements.
3. Document API usage policies for users.

## Step 6: Pre-launch Checklist
1. Verify all legal documents are in place.
2. Check compliance with tax regulations.
3. Run a comprehensive review of your website for legal disclaimers.
4. Prepare customer support resources to handle legal inquiries.
5. Schedule regular compliance reviews post-launch.

---
### Note
This guide is intended as a preliminary outline. It's recommended to consult a legal professional for specific advice related to your business.
